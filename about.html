About us

RateMyProfessorHAU is a student-driven platform designed to help Holy Angel University students share honest feedback about their professors and courses.

Our Mission

We aim to empower students by providing a platform to share their experiences and insights about professors. Our goal is to foster informed academic community where students can make better decisions regarding their education.

We believe that every Angelite, deserves the right guide towards excellence.
